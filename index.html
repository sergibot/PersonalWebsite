Bio

My research focuses on the harms that emerging deepfake technologies can cause to society and individuals.

I count myself hugely fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Shane D. Johnson, Dr. Sandy Schumann, and Prof. Lewis D. Griffin, and to have received doctoral funding from the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC) under the University College London (UCL) Centre for Doctoral Training in Cybersecurity.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed an MSc in Computer Science at UCL (2018-19) and a Bachelor's in Classics at The Queen's College, University of Oxford (2013-2017).

Main Research Topic:

Other Research Interests:
