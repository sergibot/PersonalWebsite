Sergi Bray - Bio


My research focuses on the harms that emerging deepfake technologies can cause to society and individuals.

I count myself hugely fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Shane D. Johnson and Dr. Sandy Schumann, and to have received doctoral funding from the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC) under the University College London (UCL) Centre for Doctoral Training in Cybersecurity.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed an MSc in Computer Science at UCL (2018-19) and a Bachelor's in Classics at The Queen's College, University of Oxford (2013-2017).

code/data. 		Dec 2022 Human Detection of Deepfakes
A survey of 280 participants recruited via Prolific.co; conditions: control, familiarization (n=20), one-time-advice, advice-with-reminders.
Main task: label an image as being "AI-generated" or "real", for 20 questions.
Image stimuli used: 50 real from FFHQ; 50 deepfake from StyleGAN2:FFHQ.
Control condition mean accuracy = 60%; Adv-w-rem condition mean acc (highest) = 64%. Overall mean accuracy = 62%.
One-way ANOVA suggested no statistically significant differences between groups. In other words...
None of the policy-applicable, scalable advice that we tried led to a significant improvement in human accuracy at detecting deepfake from non-deepfake images. Accuracy was low (62%, close to chance level 50%), but confidence remained high throughout (mean confidence was 6.8 in a 0-9 Likert scale).
Kimberly T. Mai, Sergi D. Bray, Toby Davies, Lewis D. Griffin Warning: Humans Cannot Reliably Detect Speech Deepfakes Pre-print. Jan 2023 Human Detection of Deepfakes
A survey of 529 participants recruited via Prolific.co; conditions: control, familiarization (n=5).
Main task: label an audio clip as being real or fake, for 20 questions.
Audio stimuli used: 50 real English clips from LJSpeech, 50 real Mandarin clips from CSMSC; 100 deepfake clips from pre-trained VITS models.
Additional split: unary versus binary choice. Mean accuracy when unary choice: 70.35% Mean accuracy when binary choice: 85.59%
